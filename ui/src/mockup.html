Rules

  1. Guard 5 cards Player designates another player and names a type of card. If that player's hand matches the type of card specified, that player is eliminated from the round. However, Guard cannot be named as the type of card.
  2. Priest 2 cards Player is allowed to see another player's hand.
  3. Baron 2 cards Player will choose another player and privately compare hands. The player with the lower-strength hand is eliminated from the round.
  4. Handmaid 2 cards Player cannot be affected by any other player's card until the next turn.
  5. Prince 2 cards Player can choose any player (including themselves) to discard their hand and draw a new one. If the discarded card is the Princess, the discarding player is eliminated.
  6. King 1 card Player trades hands with any other player.
  7. Countess 1 card If a player holds both this card and either the King or Prince card, this card must be played immediately.
  8. Princess 1 card If a player plays this card for any reason, they are eliminated from the round.
